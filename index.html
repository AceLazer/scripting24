About Me

Hello, my name is Isaiah Vallecillo, also known as Ace/AceLazerDesigns, and I graduated from the Multimedia Production in 2022 at Lethbridge Polytechnic. Since I was younger, I have had an interest in art, design, and computers, and the program I chose to study in has really helped me bring these passions together and become an effective graphic designer and illustrator. I have a good working knowledge of Adobe Illustrator, After Effects, Premiere Pro, and Photoshop, as well as Clip Studio Paint, Procreate, MediBang Paint Pro, and always learning more! It is very important to me to put forward only my best effort, and to work on my projects wholeheartedly, putting in my inspirations like animation, video games, and even music into my work. I hope to be able to create designs that will be enjoyed and can inspire others.
